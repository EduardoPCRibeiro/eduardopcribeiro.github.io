Intro

Hello there! My name is Eduardo and I am a Geologist who is just really excited about spatial data and experimenting around. I studied Geology for my bachelors degree, and during my Masters I decided to focus on seismic processing and interpretation. This took me down the rabbit hole and for the last 3 years I have been working as a Marine Data Processor.

I have been working extensively with GIS and recently took on myself to learn SQL and Python. This portfolio is mostly a sandbox for me to save any ideas that I might have had, so feel free to take a gander—and if you have any questions or ideas, my email is below.
